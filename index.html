Our Specialities

Comprehensive Eye Care Services

From routine check-ups to complex surgeries, we offer the full spectrum of ophthalmic care under one roof.

LASIK Surgery

Bladeless, painless laser vision correction. Say goodbye to glasses and contacts with our state-of-the-art femtosecond LASIK technology.

  • Blade-free procedure
  • 15-minute surgery
  • Same-day vision improvement
Learn More

Cataract Surgery

Advanced phacoemulsification with premium intraocular lens implants. Restore crystal-clear vision with our minimally invasive technique.

  • Micro-incision technique
  • Premium IOL implants
  • Quick recovery
Learn More

Retina Treatment

Expert management of diabetic retinopathy, macular degeneration, retinal detachment and other complex retinal conditions.

  • Anti-VEGF injections
  • Laser photocoagulation
  • Vitrectomy surgery
Learn More

Glaucoma Care

Early detection and comprehensive management of glaucoma with advanced diagnostic tools and surgical options to protect your optic nerve.

  • Advanced tonometry
  • Visual field testing
  • Minimally invasive surgery
Learn More

Pediatric Eye Care

Gentle, child-friendly care for young patients. Early detection and treatment of lazy eye, squint, refractive errors and more.

  • Amblyopia treatment
  • Squint correction
  • Child-safe procedures
Learn More

Cornea & Transplant

Advanced corneal procedures including DALK, DSEK, and full-thickness keratoplasty for conditions like keratoconus and corneal dystrophy.

  • Corneal transplant
  • Keratoconus treatment
  • Cross-linking (CXL)

Who We Are

Your Vision Is Our Mission

Founded in 1999, Netradeep Eye Hospital has been at the forefront of ophthalmic innovation in the region. With over 50,000 successful surgeries and a team of 40+ specialist doctors, we combine clinical excellence with compassionate patient care.

Our hospital is equipped with the most advanced diagnostic and surgical technology available, ensuring every patient receives the best possible outcome. We are NABH accredited and follow international standards of care.
